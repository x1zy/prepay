Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Prepay ("we", "our", or "the App") collects,
uses, and protects information when you use our Telegram Mini App, website, TON
Connect integration, and payment features.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the App, we may process the following information:

- Telegram account information provided by Telegram Mini Apps, such as your
  Telegram user ID, username, first name, last name, and profile photo URL.
- TON wallet information provided through TON Connect, such as your connected
  wallet address and public wallet metadata.
- Payment information related to deposits and withdrawals, including deposit
  addresses, transaction hashes, payment amounts, timestamps, currency, and
  payment status.
- Technical information such as browser type, device information, IP address,
  request logs, and error logs needed to operate and secure the App.

We do not collect your private keys, seed phrases, wallet recovery phrases, or
Telegram password. You should never share this information with anyone.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information described above to:

- identify your account inside the App;
- display your Telegram profile photo and username;
- connect your Telegram user ID to your deposit address;
- process TON deposits and withdrawals;
- show your balance, order history, and payment status;
- prevent fraud, duplicate payments, abuse, and unauthorized access;
- troubleshoot errors and improve the reliability of the App;
- comply with legal, tax, accounting, or regulatory requirements when they apply.

3. Telegram Mini Apps

The App may use Telegram Mini Apps data made available by Telegram. Telegram is
responsible for its own processing of your Telegram account data. You can review
Telegram's privacy practices in Telegram's official Privacy Policy.

4. TON Connect and Blockchain Data

When you connect a TON wallet, your wallet address and transaction activity may
be visible on the TON blockchain. Blockchain transactions are public and may be
permanent. We cannot delete or modify blockchain records.

The App never receives your private key or seed phrase. Transaction signing is
handled by your wallet provider through TON Connect.

5. Payments and Bicycle Payment Processor

The App may use a Bicycle-based payment processor to create deposit addresses,
track incoming deposits, and process withdrawals. Payment processor data may
include your internal user ID, deposit address, transaction hash, amount, and
payment status.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with:

- infrastructure providers needed to host and operate the App;
- payment-processing services used to detect and confirm blockchain payments;
- wallet and blockchain services used by your connected wallet;
- authorities or third parties when required by law or needed to protect rights,
  users, security, or the integrity of the App.

7. Data Retention

We keep information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the App,
maintain account balances and order history, prevent fraud, resolve disputes,
and meet legal or accounting obligations.

Blockchain data may remain publicly available indefinitely because it is stored
on decentralized networks outside our control.

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information.
However, no online service can guarantee absolute security. You are responsible
for securing your Telegram account, wallet, devices, passwords, and recovery
phrases.

9. Your Choices

You may stop using the App at any time. You may disconnect your wallet through
TON Connect or your wallet application. You may also contact us to request
access, correction, or deletion of information where applicable.

Some information may need to be retained for security, accounting, fraud
prevention, legal compliance, or because it exists on a public blockchain.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law.
If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be
posted at the same public URL with a new "Last updated" date.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the project owner or
support channel listed in the App.
